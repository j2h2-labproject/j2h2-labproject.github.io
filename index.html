LabProject

The cloud made for education and research

This project is a work-in-progress. Many features are not implemented yet and things are subject to change.
Your help would be appreciated!

LabProject is a virtual lab environment with the tools to make creating and managing virtual labs easy and simple to use. Build complex networks, share ideas, and collaborate with others.

Free and Open Source

And always will be. By being free and open source, LabProject can help everybody learn, build and connect without a hefty price tag or restrictive license.

Simple and Easy

The goal of LabProject is to make building even the most complex of networks as easy as possible.

Web Based

Access everything from a web browser. No extra clients. No plugins. Just a website.
